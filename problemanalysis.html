Problem Analysis

  1. Analyzing the problem

HDB vs Stocks as investments

First we want to show if it is a good choice to invest in HDB flats. Here we have the 10 year moving average of the annual percentage change for both Straits Times Index (aka STI: top 30 companies in Singapore) and hdb resale prices.

As you seen in the graph, the line for hdb resale - the red line is almost always higher than that for STI, therefore suggesting higher returns at all stages in time. We calculated the return of investment for both hdb resale and STI between 1999 and 2022. ROI for hdb resale is 1.12, while ROI for STI is only 0.63.

Flats bought over time

Next, we wanted to investigate how flats are bought over time. Agents can see that breakdown of sales for each type of flats and that 1 room, 2 room and multi generation do not contribute much to overall sales per year. We labelled the colours according to the various types to allow agents easily differentiate the different sub-groups.



Treemap based on transactions per unit

The treemap done is an ordered treemap pivoted by size. It shows that the average price of central is the highest, however, it is still being sold more often than the east region. This goes to show that there are alot more factors that go into buying a house and determining the price.




Rate of change of average prices for different regions

From the tree map, central does not seem to be the best investment to make over Jan 2018 to March 2022, however, as seen from the treemap, central has the highest average price. Hence, the general public may not know that investing in certain regions could result in better rates of return.

Average price of each town over time

This animation shows the historical and current trends for resale. It shows the top 10 most expensive towns. The resale prices of the flats were averaged out within each town. Prices increased over time but if we take price as a measure of popularity, the most popular towns change through the years. The towns and prices are clearly labelled and in a readable size. The colours are also limited to a 12 colour palette.
