Problem Analysis

  1. Analyzing the problem

HDB vs Stocks as investments

First we want to show if it is a good choice to invest in HDB flats. Here we have the 10 year moving average of the annual percentage change for both Straits Times Index (aka STI: top 30 companies in Singapore) and hdb resale prices.

As you can see, the line for hdb resale - the red line is almost always higher than that for STI, therefore suggesting higher returns at all stages in time. We calculated the return of investment for both hdb resale and STI between 1999 and 2022. ROI for hdb resale is 1.12, while ROI for STI is only 0.63.

Flats bought over time

Next, we wanted to investigate how flats are bought over time. Agents can see that breakdown of sales for each type of flats and that 1 room, 2 room and multi generation do not contribute much to overall sales per year. We labelled the colours according to the various types to allow agents easily differentiate the different sub-groups.
