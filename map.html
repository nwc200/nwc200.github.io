HDB resold flats

Layers can be found on the top right corner. This was done to increase the data ink ratio. The higher the score, the closer it is to the amenity. Hence, a large area of the star plot is desired.

Scoring: The score of the star plot is calculated by taking 5km and deducting the actual distance from the amenity. For example, a polyclinic within 1km of the HDB is rated as a 4 on the star plot. Amenities with a distance of 5km or further from the house is rated a 0.
